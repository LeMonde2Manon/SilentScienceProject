---

## Guidelines

- Choose the theme that best represents the scientific concept.
- Avoid creating new themes unless they represent a major scientific discipline.
- Highly specialized fields (e.g. ethology, entomology, ornithology, ichthyology, mineralogy) should generally be included in the closest existing theme.
- Keep the theme list stable to ensure a consistent organization of the library.
