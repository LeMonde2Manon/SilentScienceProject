# Why this license?

This project was created to expand access to science through Augmentative and Alternative Communication (AAC).

Many existing AAC pictogram libraries focus primarily on everyday communication. This project aims to make scientific knowledge accessible by providing freely available pictograms covering astronomy, biology, physics, chemistry, geology and many other fields.

## Our philosophy

Every child deserves the opportunity to ask questions such as:

- "What is a galaxy?"
- "How does DNA work?"
- "Why do volcanoes erupt?"
- "What is the Universe made of?"

Communication should not be limited by the available vocabulary.

## Why CC BY-NC-SA 4.0?

This library is released under the **Creative Commons Attribution–NonCommercial–ShareAlike 4.0 International** license.

The goal is simple:

- ✅ Families can use the pictograms freely.
- ✅ Teachers can use them in class.
- ✅ Speech therapists can integrate them into therapy.
- ✅ Schools, universities and non-profit organizations can share and improve them.
- ✅ Researchers may use them for education and accessibility projects.

However, we do not want a freely created community resource to become part of a proprietary commercial product without permission.

The **NonCommercial** clause exists to protect the work of contributors and to ensure that accessibility remains the primary objective of this project.

## Commercial use

If you wish to integrate this library into a commercial AAC application, software package, educational platform or other commercial product, please contact the project maintainer.

We strongly support collaborations that improve accessibility.

Commercial permissions may be granted on a case-by-case basis when they are consistent with the mission of the project.

## Our commitment

This project will always remain focused on one objective:

> **Making scientific knowledge accessible to AAC users, freely and collaboratively.**

If this project helps even one child ask a new question about the world, then it has achieved its purpose.
